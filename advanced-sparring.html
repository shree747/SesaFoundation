Advanced Training Skills

What the Master Teaches at Advanced Level

Advanced sparring goes far beyond kicks and punches. The master focuses on strategy, speed, reading opponents, and elite competition conditioning.

⚡

Speed & Explosive Power

Plyometric drills and reaction training push students to deliver techniques 30% faster than at beginner level, using full body kinetic energy.

🦅

Spinning & Jump Kicks

Spinning heel kick (Dwi Huryeo Chagi), jump back kick, and tornado kick — high-risk, high-scoring techniques used in Olympic competition.

🧠

Fight IQ & Opponent Reading

The master trains students to study body language, identify patterns, and bait opponents into mistakes — the mental edge that wins close matches.

🛡️

Advanced Defence & Counters

Slipping, stepping out, and simultaneous counter-attacks. Blocking is the last resort — evading and countering in one movement is the goal.

🎯

Scoring Zone Precision

Olympic-rule scoring targets (trunk and head) are drilled with electronic gear. Students learn exactly which techniques score the most points.

💪

Match Conditioning

3-round fight simulation, interval training, and footwork ladders build the endurance needed to maintain aggression and accuracy for a full match.

Training Methodology

How the Master Trains Advanced Students

"At this level, technique is assumed. I am now training your mind, your timing, and your will. These are what separate a fighter from a champion." — Master, Sesa Foundation Taekwondo

Advanced sessions are intense and unpredictable by design. The master introduces live sparring scenarios, random opponent switching, and video review so students can see their own blind spots.

Students train with electronic scoring vests so every technique that lands is measured objectively. This removes guesswork and drives precision.

Partner rotation is mandatory — fighters must adapt to different heights, ranges, and styles every round to develop true tournament readiness.

1

Shadow Sparring

Solo mirror work — visualise an opponent and execute full combinations at speed.

2

Pad & Target Drills

Precision striking with focus mitts held at competition scoring angles and heights.

3

Live Sparring Rounds

Full-contact Olympic-rule rounds with electronic gear. Coach gives inter-round feedback.

4

Video Analysis

Footage reviewed with the master to identify weaknesses and refine strategy.

5

Conditioning Finish

Every session ends with stamina and explosive power circuits to build fight endurance.

Competition Pathway

How the Master Gets Students to High-Level Matches

Advanced students follow a structured 4-stage competition pathway designed to peak performance at the right moment.

Stage 1 — Weeks 1–4

Internal Elite Sparring

Advanced students spar exclusively with the top fighters at Sesa Foundation. High pressure, full contact, Olympic rules — this is the baseline standard every student must reach before going external.

Stage 2 — Weeks 5–8

Inter-Academy Challenge Matches

The master arranges formal challenge matches with high-level academies in the region. These are structured bouts with a referee, scoreboard, and full gear — real match pressure without championship stakes.

Stage 3 — Weeks 9–12

State & National Open Tournaments

Students are entered into WTF-affiliated state and national open events. The master travels with the team as licensed coach, strategising between rounds and reviewing every result.

Stage 4 — Weeks 13–16

Elite Grading & Black Belt Pathway

Top performers are assessed for Dan (black belt) grading. This includes advanced form performance, breaking, and a live sparring grading round judged by external examiners.

Advanced Core Values

Power · Precision · Mentality

At advanced level, the master focuses on three upgraded fighting virtues that go beyond the basics — these define a competitor.

💥

POWER

Not brute force — but the ability to generate maximum impact with perfect technique, timing, and hip rotation in a fraction of a second.

  • Full body rotation on every strike
  • Kihap (battle cry) to maximise force transfer
  • Core and plyometric conditioning 3× per week
  • Breaking board training for power benchmarking
🎯

PRECISION

Scoring a point requires landing on a specific target in a specific way. Precision is drilled until the right technique hits the right zone every time, under pressure.

  • Electronic hogu scoring gear in every session
  • Target zone drills — trunk and head scoring
  • Counter-attack timing within 0.5 seconds
  • Olympic rulebook studied and applied in drills
🧠

MENTALITY

Champions are not broken by setbacks. The master trains students to stay calm when losing, aggressive when winning, and focused no matter the score.

  • Visualisation before every sparring session
  • Controlled breathing between rounds
  • Losing is treated as data — never an identity
  • Win or lose, debrief with the master always follows
